about

me

I have begun my journey through the depths of the audio industry as a musician. I've always been fascinated by sounds so this was a perfect chance to explore audio from another perspective.

I quickly got hooked. Being able to manipulate audio the way I wanted to achieve the results I wanted seemed like magic. Soon I realized that I wanted to dive a level deeper and expand my knowledge of not only music but also sound as a whole.

After countless of composed pieces, polished tracks and creative experiences this passion remains with me. There is a lot more to learn and a lot more challenges to face. And for this, I am grateful.
